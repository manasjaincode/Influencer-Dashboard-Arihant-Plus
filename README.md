Overview
The Arihant Influencer Dashboard is a high-performance ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between financial content creators and the stockbroking world. This platform empowers influencers to monetize their audience by streamlining the process of lead generation, account opening (Demat), and performance tracking.

By leveraging personalized referral engines, influencers can onboard users to Arihant Capital, earning sustainable brokerage sharing and performance-based rewards.

Business Value & Strategic Impact
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Optimization: Instead of high-burn traditional ads, this "Pay-per-Result" model ensures Arihant Capital only pays for successful conversions, significantly lowering the overall CAC.

Scalable Distribution Network: Turn every financial blogger, YouTuber, and Instagrammer into a digital branch office, rapidly expanding Arihant's footprint across diverse demographics.

Passive Revenue Streams for Creators: Influencers gain access to a reliable, long-term revenue model through brokerage sharing, fostering a loyal partnership between the brand and the creator.

Data-Driven Decision Making: The dashboard provides real-time analytics on lead-to-conversion ratios, allowing both the brand and the influencer to optimize their marketing strategies.

Key Features
Personalized Referral Engine: Instant generation of unique tracking links for Demat account opening.

Real-time Lead Tracking: Transparent funnel visibility from "Link Clicked" to "Account Activated."

Earnings & Payout Management: Automated calculation of brokerage sharing, referral bonuses, and monthly payouts.

Marketing Toolkit: Access to pre-approved brand assets, banners, and educational content to maintain brand compliance.

Performance Analytics: Deep insights into audience behavior and conversion heatmaps.
